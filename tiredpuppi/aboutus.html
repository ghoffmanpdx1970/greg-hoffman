About Us

We are two little girl puppies living in El Paso Texas. We were born around late May/Early June of 2017, but we were abandoned in a hot apartment by our mom's humans. Fortunately, we ended up being rescued at the end of June by our new mom and dad, and we're glad that we have a real home! Our people love us, and we love them. We are still little, so of course we make our people mad at us with the puppy things that we do like having accidents (or on purposes), tearing up stuff, play fighting with each other, and recently we learned how to get on the dining room table to help ourselves to delicious people food!

We don't know how often our dad will update this site for us, so please be patient if it's been a while since anything new has been added. OH! And please don't laugh at him, he's still learning how to do all this Interwebs stuff.

If you want to contact us, we do have our own email addresses, just look in the "Contact Us" link. We don't know WHY we have email addresses, we can't read or write. But, for some reason, dad thought it would be a good idea... weirdo.
